# Learning Process

## Question 1
**What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.**

The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you understand a topic deeply by explaining it in simple words as if teaching someone else.

## Question 3
**What are active and diffused modes of thinking?**

1. **Focused Mode**  
   Focused mode is when we concentrate deeply on a problem or task. It helps in learning detailed concepts and solving problems step by step.

2. **Diffused Mode**  
   Diffused mode happens when the mind is relaxed, such as during walking, sleeping, or resting. It helps connect ideas creatively and understand the bigger picture.
